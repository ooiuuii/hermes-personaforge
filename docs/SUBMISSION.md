## Discord Draft

Project: Hermes PersonaForge

This is my fourth standalone submission in the same Hermes business-agent story:

- Hermes Foundry: enterprise approval orchestration
- Hermes Sourcing Desk: procurement sourcing and savings proof
- Hermes Commerce Ops: paid e-commerce order operations
- Hermes PersonaForge: paid persona-pack entitlement and runtime delivery

The product idea is simple: people do not only want productivity from AI agents; sometimes they also want emotional value. PersonaForge turns a purchased character/persona pack into a licensed voice-and-vision companion session.

Demo loop:

- A customer buys an AI Companion Persona Pack on the Qiance EC site.
- Hermes verifies the redacted paid-order evidence.
- Hermes checks delivery economics before spending runtime effort.
- Hermes unlocks a hashable persona manifest download.
- The buyer imports that manifest into a Shinsekai-compatible local runtime.
- The runtime inspects the product/order page, detects self-reference, and switches from surprised to shy with a voice response.
- `npm test` regenerates payment reconciliation, persona margin gate, license manifest, runtime trace, safety report, audit ledger, and proof manifest.

Important boundary: the submitted repo does not redistribute third-party character packs, sprites, voice checkpoints, model weights, local runtime code, customer PII, or payment credentials. It ships the business workflow and verifier-safe proof artifacts.
